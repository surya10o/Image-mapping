CHRIST THE REDEEMER
  1. Christ the redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, created by French sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa

  2. Location:Corcovado mountain, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

  3. Material:Soapstone

  4. Width:28 metres (92 ft

  5. Height:30 metres (98 ft)
